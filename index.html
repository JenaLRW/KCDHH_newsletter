📋 News & Policy
Federal

New FCC video conferencing rules

The FCC adopted new rules requiring all video conferencing platforms to support accurate captioning, sign language interpreter display, and compatibility with assistive technology — ensuring virtual meetings are accessible for everyone, including people who are deaf or hard of hearing.
