Federal

House Bill 164

Kentucky HB 164 is a law relating to hearing aid coverage. It raises the insurance reimbursement cap for hearing aids from $1,400 to $2,500 and establishes network adequacy requirements for hearing aid services. It was signed by the Governor on April 3, 2026, and takes effect January 1, 2027.
